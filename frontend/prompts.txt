Q14. Wire all registration forms directly to Supabase.
A:   Each registration inserts into two tables:
     - Employer   → users (role='employer')  + employers

     - Faculty    → users (role='faculty')   + faculties
     - Student    → users (role='student')   + students
     On success: fields cleared, redirect to /auth/login.
